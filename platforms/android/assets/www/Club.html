Chippa United is a South African football club based in Port Elizabeth, Eastern Cape. Affectionately known as the "Chilli Boys", the team is wholly-owned by Chippa Investment Holdings.

The club was founded in June 2010 by Chairman Siviwe "Chippa" Mpengesi. Mr Mpengesi bought the Castle League franchise of Mbekweni Cosmos which later changed to Chippa United FC.

In their first season in the Vodacom League, Chilli Boys were crowned the Vodacom Promotional Champions by winning the play-offs in June 2011. This accomplishment ensured their promotion, into the National First Division (NFD). The club finished their debut season in the National First Division in 2nd place and qualified for the play-offs. They won the play-offs and that saw them gain promotion to the Premier Soccer League.

The Chilli Boys were relegated after their first season in the top flight. They played in the NFD during the 2013/14 season. They won the championship and earned direct promotion back into the PSL.

Canterbury is the apparel partner for the 2016/17 season.
